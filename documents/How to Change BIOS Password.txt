Check if NuGet is installed and what version:
Get-PackageProvider -Name NuGet -ListAvailable
 
Force upgrade NuGet to latest version:
Install-PackageProvider -Name NuGet -Force

Check if DellBIOSProvider is installed and available versions:
Get-Module -Name DellBIOSProvider -ListAvailable | Select-Object Name, Version, Path

Force upgrade DellBIOSProvider to latest version:
Update-Module -Name DellBIOSProvider -Force
Install-Module -Name DellBIOSProvider -Force -AllowClobber
